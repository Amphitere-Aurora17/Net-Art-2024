A Neverending Dream

A never-ending dream, a dream of you

  1. I'm waiting for the night,

  2. Drifting Away,

  3. In the waves of my dreams,

  4. To another day

  5. I'm standing on the hill

  6. And beyond the clouds

  7. The wind is blowing still

  8. And catching my doubts

    A never-ending dream, a dream of you
  • I believe I receive a sign of you

  • Tonight I want to hide my feelings to

  • As you do, and I wanna be with you
